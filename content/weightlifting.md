The sport of Olympic weightlifting.
The entire sport consists of two movements:
- The [[snatch]]
- The [[clean and jerk]]

It's a beautiful and misunderstood sport. On the surface, it looks simple. Just move the bar from the floor to above your head. How hard can it be? However, after spending over 6 years doing two movements, I'm still learning new things about the movements, and refining my technique.

>"Weightlifting’s beauty comes in its simplicity. A bar lying on a platform, two lifts, six attempts. After a meet, everyone knows who the best is, and by how much. We can measure our success in kilograms. At the end of the day, Weightlifting is all about the numbers." - ProleWL
