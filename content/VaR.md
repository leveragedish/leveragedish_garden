Value at Risk, aka what’s the worst possible outcome that we can model. Typically people will do something like, what’s the worst loss in 95% of situations, or 99% of the time. Key words on “model”, and 95%, or 99%. Note that the latter leave out tails (which should really, probably not be ignored).
