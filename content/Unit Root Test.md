A Unit Root Test is a test of [[stationarity]] that’s designed to determine whether [[differencing]] is required. It determines the existence of a unit root, which is a property that signals random walk behavior.

Differencing is a technique that’s used to turn time series into stationary data. It removes trends or seasonality by subtracting the previous observation from the current one.
