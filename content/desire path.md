Desire paths, social trails, intention lines, etc. Are informal foot paths that people create. You see them all the time in places where sidewalks are built on top of dirt/grassy areas. The telltale signs of desire paths are trodden down lines where the grass has been eroded into dirt, often showcasing a more efficient path towards a common destination than the concrete ones that have been laid out.
