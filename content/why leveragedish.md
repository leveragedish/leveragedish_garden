Leveragedish. As in, kind of leveraged. Leveraged-ish, ya know?
I named my site this because I think it accurately describes my lifestyle. I’ve grown over the years to understand the importance of taking risk, betting on yourself, and levering up to be able to get somewhere that’s beyond where you yourself could possibly reach on your own. Also, as a trader, leverage is something that's very directly relevant to my career.

Einstein said "Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world. He who understands it, earns it … he who doesn’t … pays it.”. Well, I think leverage is the 9th wonder of the world.

In its most literal sense, a lever is “a rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to help move a heavy or firmly fixed load with one end when pressure is applied to the other.” 

>“You can't move a rock by your self. You just need a lever to change the direction of that rock.   
That is smart work.” - M.Rehan Behleem
