- How to read for learning
- Making the most out of your books
	- Tackling the chapters, wrestling with the content, asking the hard questions
	- Don’t just take notes word for word, make them your own. The process of filtering the words through your brain and forming your own is building connections in your mind that are lasting; instead of words flowing in and out mindlessly. 
- Reading is NOT a passive activity (if you want it to be)
- You bought all these books, grow from them. 
- After each chapter, write a summary, write your thoughts and opinions on the chapter. Do you agree with the author? Do you disagree? Why? What did you like/dislike? What questions does this raise? Talk about it. Take your time. Read, reread.
