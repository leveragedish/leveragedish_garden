Rather than having a handful of folders or some larger structured organization, I'm going to test out using tags to be able to cluster some of these notes. 
In Quartz, you can hover over or click on a hashtag to take you to a page collection of similar tags.

Some more unique tags to my digital garden that demand some explanation:

#meta 
Things tagged with meta are notes that are about the site itself and how it works. 

#self 
The self tag is put on notes that don't quite fit in #philosophy but are still comments on topics that I felt are more reflective and practical. I don't quite have a good definition yet of what these should really be, but there is some common thread tying them all together. probably.

#nugget 
The nugget tag is for things that I simply enjoy. Everybody loves nuggets.
