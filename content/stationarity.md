A stationary time series is a time series where the properties don’t depend on the time when the series is observed. So time series data with trends, or seasonality, are not stationary. White noise is stationary, it doesn’t matter when you look at it. 

A stationary time series will have no predictable patterns long term. Time plots will show the series to be roughly horizontal with constant variance.
